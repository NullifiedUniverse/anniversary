# Initial Concept
InstaMemories: A digital scrapbook using Gemini AI to turn Instagram chat data into a narrative experience with interactive analytics and relationship insights.

# Product Definition - InstaMemories

## 🌟 Vision
InstaMemories is a deeply personal digital scrapbook created exclusively for **Null (NullifiedGalaxy)** and **Yun (Vanessa)** to celebrate their first anniversary. It transforms their Instagram chat history into a heartwarming, romantic narrative using Gemini AI, focusing on the milestones, shared patterns, and the beautiful growth of their connection over the past year.

## 🎯 Target Users
- **Null (NullifiedGalaxy) & Yun (Vanessa):** This project is a bespoke experience for their first anniversary, focusing on their unique story.

## ✨ Core Features
- **AI Storytelling:** Gemini-powered narrative synthesis that crafts a deeply sweet and emotional summary of Null and Yun's first year together.
- **Interactive Analytics:** Visual charts (using Recharts) for message frequency, active hours, and response time patterns, revealing the rhythm of their relationship.
- **Sentiment Cloud:** A physics-based word cloud visualizing the most frequently used words in their chat, capturing their shared language.
- **Memory Exploration:** High-performance search and first-message discovery to instantly revisit the moment it all began.

## 🔒 Privacy & Security
- **Zero-Knowledge Architecture:** All parsing and analysis happen strictly client-side. Their personal chat data never leaves the browser.
- **Secure AI Sampling:** Only temporary, cleaned snippets are sent to Gemini for narrative generation, ensuring their privacy is paramount.

## 🎨 Emotional Goal
- **Deeply Sweet & Romantic:** The experience should feel like a digital love letter, emphasizing nostalgia, emotional depth, and the beauty of their first year together.
- **Very Emotional:** Every interaction should evoke the warmth and connection they share, making it a truly special anniversary gift.
