# Product Guidelines - InstaMemories

## 🖋️ Prose Style
- **Sweet & Romantic:** The language should be deeply romantic, focusing on shared memories, milestones, and the emotional connection between Null and Yun.
- **Narrative & Flowing:** AI-generated stories and UI text should flow naturally like a narrative, creating a cohesive and sentimental story of their relationship.
- **Warm & Intimate:** Every interaction should feel like a personal digital love letter, emphasizing their bond and growth together.

## 🎨 Branding & UX
- **Dreamy & Soft Aesthetic:** Use a dreamy and soft visual style with gentle transitions and animations (using Framer Motion) to create a sentimental atmosphere.
- **Personalized (Nicknames) 💖:** Incorporate Null and Yun's personal nicknames and shared inside jokes throughout the UI to make it feel truly their own.
- **Elegant & Sentimental:** Every design choice should contribute to a sense of emotional depth and nostalgic celebration.

## 🧠 Tone of Insights
- **Celebratory & Positive:** Relationship insights and AI-generated "awards" should celebrate their first year together and the positive growth of their connection.
- **Deeply Emotional:** Insights should reflect the genuine and deep emotional bond they share, making the data feel meaningful and heartfelt.
- **Narrative-Driven Analytics:** Charts and visualizations (using Recharts) should not just be data points but should tell a part of their story.

## 🛠️ Interaction Design
- **Gentle & Fluid:** All UI interactions should be smooth and fluid, avoiding jarring changes and emphasizing a gentle, nostalgic flow.
- **Interactive Storytelling:** Allow the user to "walk" through their chat history as a narrative, making the exploration an engaging and emotional journey.
